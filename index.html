In Bangladesh, six seasons with their distinctive robes come in procession, play and depart. Through there is intense heat in summer, there are the gifts of delicious fruits. Though there are floods during the rainy season. It is the season that blesses us with greenery, in autumn there is the game of hiding and seeks among the clouds, the blue sky and enchanting moonlight at night. In winter fields full of winter crops and golden paddy, ready for reaping, present a panoramic view.
